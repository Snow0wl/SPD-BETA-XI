Sigma Phi Delta Beta Xi Chapter

We are

Sigma Phi Delta emphasizes professional networking, mentorship, and community service, helping members develop both technical and interpersonal skills that are valuable in engineering careers. They also uphold values of honor, integrity, and a commitment to advancing the engineering profession. The fraternity has chapters at universities across North America and hosts various events, including career fairs, leadership workshops, and technical seminars.
