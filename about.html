About Us

Sigma Phi Delta is a professional fraternity for men in the fields of engineering and technology, with a focus on creating a supportive and engaging community for students pursuing technical careers. The Beta Xi chapter is one of the few West Virginia fraternities dedicated exclusively to the engineering discipline, which makes it unique within both the academic and professional worlds.

Our Mission

The Object of the Fraternity shall be to promote the advancement of the Engineering Profession; to foster the advancement of Engineering Education; to instill a greater spirit of cooperation among Engineering Students and Organizations; to inculcate in its members the highest ideals of virtuous manhood, good Citizenship, obedience to Law, and Brotherhood; and to encourage excellence in scholarship.

Our Vision

Sigma Phi Delta envisions a future where it stands as a global leader in engineering excellence, supporting the academic and professional growth of engineers while driving positive change within the field. Through enhanced professional development programs, Sigma Phi Delta seeks to equip members with cutting-edge skills in emerging technologies and leadership, preparing them to meet the challenges of an evolving industry. The fraternity is committed to building a strong global network of alumni who actively mentor current members, fostering lifelong connections that extend beyond college. Additionally, Sigma Phi Delta plans to increase its involvement in impactful community service projects, raising awareness of engineering’s potential to benefit society. By collaborating with industry leaders, the fraternity aspires to offer members exclusive opportunities for internships, research, and career advancement, positioning itself as a dynamic organization that promotes leadership, innovation, and ethical responsibility in engineering.
